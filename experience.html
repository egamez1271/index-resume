My Experience

My experience started in a mall ice cream shop. There was only one person there at a time who needed to run the shop and make any orders that came up. There I was able to gain the attributes of patience and hard work.

From the mall ice cream shop, I started working at a Chipotle. There I learned great discipline as I needed to wake up early and help prep all of the vegetables and food that we would serve. In addition to the discipline I learned a lot of timing skills as I was the one who stocked all of the ingredients.

After I worked at Chipotle for almost a year, I went on a full time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. While I was there, I learned a lot about customer service. I learned how to talk to people and how to showcase great qualities of what I was selling.

When I got back from my full time mission, I started working at the Cougareat where I am currently an Office Assistant. There I have brought all of those skills together and learned how to help others through their issues and I've learned a lot about how to properly use technology and forms to further business work.
